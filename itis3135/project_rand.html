Project Assignment A - Webpage with Requirements & Design for Proposed Project


Project Overview

Describe your application -

This webpage is going to be used by client Aarushi Katoch, who has a small business. The name of this business is called 'The Red', they sell unisex apparel and accessories. All of these products will be available on her site to peruse and place orders.

Intended Users of the Website -

Intended users of this site are people who are interested in buying clothing and accessories designed personally by Aarushi.

Content of the Website -

This site is going to have information about The Red and its business/owner. It will have contact information and a short gist of why the business was created and how it operates. It will have pricing information on all the products, which will be listed throughotu the site.
